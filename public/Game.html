Story So Far


The world is dark and almost silent. Your cart's wheels startle a flock of birds away. Heck, your own shadow startles you. A pair of little squirrels stare. They vanish in the forest, as if they had just remembered something important to do.


This was not the agreed upon plan. The Space Colonization Bureau had reassured you that this exoplanet was the closest candidate to Earth's condition in Canis Major Dwarf, but nothing around you seems familiar. Unfortunately, you lost sight of the rest of the crew after landing. You check your radio: its aluminium and steel material is badly damaged from the landing. You also check the most vital asset in your equipment: your Exploration Lantern of Light. Miraculously, it has been undamaged from the landing. Without it, you cannot continue on, you recall from your Space Colonization Training. Dangerous exo-lifeforms known from previous explorers have been known to prey on those without their Light. Keep your Exploration Lantern of Light filled with any consummable resource you can find.

Time is critical: find a way to repair your radio comms.
